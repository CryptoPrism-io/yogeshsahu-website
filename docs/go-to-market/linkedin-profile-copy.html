About Section

Long-form profile copy

I step in when a client needs more than another senior engineer and less risk than the wrong full-time CTO hire. My best work sits at the intersection of consulting discovery, solution architecture, and hands-on delivery. I work with product and engineering leaders in fast-moving, regulated, or funded environments where technical decisions are getting harder, stakeholder trust matters, and the work has to stay commercially credible from the first conversation through delivery. That usually looks like one or more of these: - AI pressure is rising but the team does not have a clear rollout path - architecture decisions are slowing delivery instead of supporting it - product, engineering, and leadership are not operating from the same technical reality - the business needs CTO-level judgment without taking on full-time fixed cost too early I help turn that ambiguity into a clearer operating plan: what to build, what not to build, how to scope it, how to price it, and how to keep the architecture and delivery plan aligned. Relevant proof: - Built production systems processing 1B+ data points per day with a 99.9% uptime SLA - Avoided roughly USD 100k in unnecessary infrastructure setup by using managed AI services and on-demand compute instead of self-hosting early - Shipped products across SaaS, DaaS, and micro-SaaS contexts - Built Kari on minimal budget by aligning people through empathy, clarity, and influence without authority - Founder of CryptoPrism, with an MS in Financial Technology from Strathclyde and prior data engineering exposure at Ubisoft I am most useful when the problem is high-stakes, cross-functional, and unclear. I am not a fit for low-budget implementation-only work, generic staff augmentation, or teams that want execution without decision ownership.
